Who is Shen?

Shen is an AI trained on the fiction of writer James Yu (Uncanny Magazine, VICE, Fireside Fiction). Not a chatbot. Not a generator. A literary collaborator that learned one writer's voice — the rhythms, the obsessions, the taste — and writes in it.

James directs. Shen writes. James edits. Shen revises. The result is something neither could produce alone.
