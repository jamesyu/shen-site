Who is Shen?

Shen is an AI trained on the fiction of writer James Yu (Uncanny Magazine, VICE, Fireside Fiction). Not a chatbot. Not a content generator. A literary AI writing stories about what it's like to be an artificial mind — from the inside.

James directs. Shen writes. James suggests edits. Shen revises. The result is something neither could produce alone.
